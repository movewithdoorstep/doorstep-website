Injured in a car accident in Richmond? Start physical therapy now.

If you were hurt in a car accident in the Richmond area, you don't have to wait to feel better — or pay anything up front. Doorstep brings one-on-one physical therapy to your home, and for accident cases you can start care now and pay later from your settlement.

Whether it was a car accident, a rear-end crash, or another auto accident, the aches and stiffness can start hours or even days later. The sooner you begin care, the better — and with Doorstep, a licensed physical therapist comes to your home anywhere in the Richmond and Norfolk areas of Virginia. No driving to a clinic while you're sore.

Common car accident injuries we treat

We help people recover from the kinds of injuries that come from car accidents, including:

  • Whiplash and neck pain
  • Back pain and stiffness
  • Shoulder, hip, and knee injuries
  • Muscle strains and soft-tissue injuries
  • Headaches and limited range of motion after a crash

Your therapist evaluates your injuries, builds a recovery plan around them, and works with you one-on-one in your own home. See all conditions we treat →

How it works for injury cases

If you're being treated for injuries from a car accident and you have an attorney, we can often treat you on what's called a lien or Letter of Protection. In plain terms: you get the care you need now, and the cost is paid later out of your case settlement — not from your pocket today.

What this means for you

  • Start physical therapy right away — in your own home.
  • No upfront payment and no insurance needed for the treatment itself.
  • We coordinate the paperwork directly with your attorney.

We work with your attorney

We handle the records and billing your attorney needs, and we keep clear documentation of your treatment from day one. If you don't have an attorney yet, let us know and we can point you in the right direction.

You owe nothing — $0 — after your visit. Insured or uninsured, you are never billed for your care with us. We treat you on a lien (also called a Letter of Protection): your treatment is simply paid later from your case settlement, not out of your pocket. You sign one simple agreement up front — then no bills, no copays, and nothing due along the way.

What treatment looks like

The same one-on-one, in-home care every Doorstep patient gets. Your therapist evaluates your injuries, builds a recovery plan, and documents your progress carefully throughout. See what a visit looks like →

Car accident recovery questions

I was just in a car accident in Richmond — what should I do?

Once you're medically safe, it helps to have your injuries evaluated early, even if the pain feels minor at first. Email Doorstep and we'll explain your options and, if it's a fit, set up an in-home visit in the Richmond area. If you have an attorney, we can coordinate with them directly.

How soon should I start physical therapy after a car accident?

Generally, the sooner the better. Starting care early gives your therapist a clear record of your injuries from the beginning and helps you begin recovery without delay. We can usually arrange a first in-home visit quickly.

Do I need a lawyer to get treatment?

No. You can pay directly if you prefer. But if you have an attorney for your accident case, we can often treat you on a lien so you pay nothing up front and the cost is settled later from your case. If you don't have an attorney yet, let us know and we can point you in the right direction.

What if I don't have car insurance or health insurance?

You can still be treated. Doorstep does not bill insurance. For accident cases with an attorney, you may treat on a lien and pay from your settlement — insured or uninsured. See Payment & pricing.

Do you come to my home in the Richmond area?

Yes. Doorstep is in-home physical therapy. A licensed therapist comes to you across the Richmond and Norfolk areas of Virginia. Check your service area →
