For personal-injury attorneys

In-home physical therapy for your clients, with the documentation and reliability your cases require.

Doorstep Physical Therapy delivers one-on-one, in-home outpatient care to injured clients who need treatment now. We understand that the quality of our documentation and the timeliness of our records affect your case — and we treat that responsibility seriously.

What we provide your firm

Defensible documentation

Thorough initial evaluations, objective measures, consistent progress notes, and clear discharge summaries — built to withstand scrutiny.

Timely records & billing

Records and itemized billing delivered on the timeline your case management requires, with responsive follow-up on requests.

Lien / LOP handling

We treat qualifying clients on a lien or Letter of Protection and coordinate financial documentation directly with your office.

MoveDocs integration

Billing, records, and case funding are managed through the MoveDocs platform for a clean, trackable workflow.

PENDING ATTORNEY REVIEW — do not publish as-is. This is how Doorstep gets paid without insurance. Instead of billing now, Doorstep treats on credit and is repaid out of the eventual settlement or judgment: The patient signs a lien agreement assigning a portion of their settlement to cover Doorstep's bill. The attorney signs an LOP acknowledging the lien and agreeing to protect (hold back and pay) Doorstep's balance from settlement proceeds before disbursing to the client. No upfront cost to the patient — they pay from the case outcome. Doorstep carries the risk. If the case loses or settles low, repayment may be reduced or not happen. Your agreements need to spell out what happens then (e.g., whether the patient remains responsible, how reductions are handled).
